Agriculture

Sustainable and Profitable

PLANTAR

The main challenges in agriculture is to fulfil the food demands of a constantly growing population while protecting the economical, ecological, and social resources. Controlled-environment agriculture (CEA), such as vertical farming or greenhouses, addresses this challenge by maximizing crop production in reduced areas using automatic or semi-automatic processes under controlled conditions. We are specialist in CEA. Our intelligent platform PLANTAR combines proximal sensing, robotics, and artificial intelligence for selecting the optimal environmental parameters (light, humidity, temperature) and the best irrigation and fertigation strategies for each individual plant, maximizing sustainability while reducing costs.

Process automation using advance sensing and robotics.

Maximal yield production with minimal resources.

ANY TIME
ANY WHERE

PLANTAR can be accessed through an interactive and intuitive web interface. You can check the current treatments applied to each plant and the plants' history in your indoor farm at any time and from any place in the world.
